About Biomedical IQ

Ensuring Lifelong Health Through Equipment Care.

BioMedical IQ is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to enhance the efficiency and reliability of medical equipment maintenance in healthcare facilities. We provide a seamless way for hospitals and medical centers to manage their equipment inventory, ensuring optimal functionality through preventive and corrective maintenance.

Our platform empowers biomedical engineers with real-time data, automated alerts, and detailed analytics to minimize equipment downtime and ensure timely interventions, helping healthcare providers deliver uninterrupted, quality care.
